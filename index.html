Features

Match Threads

Ladder bot will create threads for each match, notifying captains and handling game results.

Notifications

Captains are always kept in the loop with automatic notifications sent to their designated notification thread. They receive updates on new team sign-ups, players joining or leaving their team, as well as important updates from moderators and more.

Simple to use

The most used commands are in the form of buttons. Making it simple for captains to do what they want.

Lots More

Including, player invites, help commands, spectator mode, full set of moderation commands, custom roles, team stats, and API access!
